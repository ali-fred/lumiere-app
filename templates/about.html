ℹ️ About Lumière Du Progrès

Lumière Du Progrès ni application ya wallet ifasha abakoresha kubika, kohereza no kwakira amafaranga mu buryo bworoshe kandi bwizewe.

Irimwo features zirimo wallet management, transaction history, sending & receiving funds, hamwe n’indi mirimo y’ingenzi ifasha abakoresha.

🛣️ Roadmap (Inyongera zigiye kuza)

  • 📱 Mobile App (Android & iOS)
  • 🔐 2FA Security (Two Factor Authentication)
  • 🌍 Multi-currency support
  • 💳 Integration ya Mobile Money (Lumicash, Ecocash, n’abandi)
  • 🤝 Referral & earning system
  • 📊 Advanced analytics dashboard
  • 💬 Live support chat
